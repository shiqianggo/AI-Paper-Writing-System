```
**3 | RESULTS**

**3.1 | Phenotypic Variations Across Colonization Years**

To investigate the temporal dynamics of _Spartina alterniflora_ invasion, we established a sampling design in the estuarine area of the Zhangjiangkou Mangrove National Nature Reserve. Using remote-sensing satellite imagery to determine the timing of patch appearance, the local population was categorized into four distinct colonization-year patches (2012, 2013, 2014, and 2015), which were sampled across three microgeographic zones (estuary, competition, and dense zones) (**Fig 1a, Fig S1a**). Phenotypic measurements were conducted within randomly placed quadrats to evaluate fundamental growth and reproductive features, including biomass allocations and morphological traits for intact plants and mature inflorescences. An analysis of the acquired phenotypic results demonstrated significant multidimensional variations corresponding to the establishment timing of the patches. Specifically, aboveground organ trait indices, including stem biomass, shoot biomass, leaf biomass, and inflorescence biomass, exhibited significant differences among the four patches defined by colonization years (**Fig 1b–e**). Parallel to these biomass allocations, growth-related morphology and reproductive metrics—namely flowering stem density, plant height, inflorescence length, and spikelet number—also displayed significant spatial-temporal divergence across the chronosequence (**Fig S1b–e**). Overall, these phenotypic patterns highlight that _S. alterniflora_ patches undergo significant shifts in structural resource allocation and reproductive output traversing different colonization years.

**3.2 | Transcriptomic and Metabolomic Dynamics**

We integrated transcriptomic and metabolomic analyses to systematically investigate the underlying molecular differences among the colonization-year patches. To maximize annotation accuracy prior to analysis, a previously published _S. alterniflora_ genome was utilized as a functional template and successfully re-annotated through the InterPro database, resulting in a total of `***` tightly co-annotated core genes.

For transcriptomic profiling, total RNA extracted from snap-frozen leaf tissues was sequenced via the Illumina NovaSeq 6000 platform, generating robust datasets mapped against the reference genome that identified a total of `***` expressed genes with verified mapping statistics (**Table S3**). Principal component analysis (PCA) mapped to normalized expression counts displayed robust structural clusters, establishing substantial genome-wide transcriptomic divergence correlated with the varying colonization years (**Fig 2a**). Utilizing the Mfuzz minimum centroid distance to assign classification parameters (**Fig S2a**), transcripts were successfully partitioned into four distinct temporal expression trends representing ordered response behaviors (**Fig S2c**). It was explicitly identified that 12,026, 10,033, 5,453, and 10,196 high-confidence core genes drove these four respective time-scale clusters. Detailed Gene Ontology (GO) pathway enrichment mapping for these highly expressed core associations established a comprehensive transition of physiological functionalities partitioned specifically to each colonization-year environment (2012 through 2015) (**Fig 2c–f**), prominently featuring mechanisms related to `[Data Required]`. Collectively, the temporal transcriptomics mapping reveals a highly structured functional shift governed by specific gene-regulatory programs modulating adaptative responses following patch establishment.

Complementary to these patterns, an untargeted LC-MS-based metabolomic profiling isolated 68,574 unique MS2 features, yielding 1,107 structurally annotated features within the leaf components, of which 143 confident targets mapped cleanly to established KEGG identifiers (**Table S5**). Following quality regulation through the directed exclusion of anomalous batch interactions, PCA representation generated highly discrete metabolic profiles clearly tracking the sequential spatial–temporal distribution arrays across the established zones (**Fig 2b**). Subsequently employing similar minimum distance classifications (**Fig S2b**), differential clustering generated four distinct metabolite trajectory groups distributed over the chronosequence (**Fig S2d**). Pathway enrichment evaluations mapped discrete groups of the significantly changing constituent compounds to key KEGG signaling networks varying significantly throughout the lifespan stages of the colonization locations (**Fig 2g**), including `[Data Required]`. Ultimately, these dynamic spatial shifts emphasize robust and tightly organized local metabolomic reprogramming across the chronosequence timeline.

**3.3 | Genetic Architecture and Multi-omics Integration**

To elucidate the baseline genetic structure facilitating these biological responses, we characterized SNP-level variations across the microgeographic sampling gradient. Evaluations detailing pair-wise interrelatedness revealed highly localized patterns of spatial kinship segregation mapping to localized patches (**Fig 3a**). Utilizing filtered, highly informative genome loci generated by isolating the intersecting sets of outlier _F_ST_ differentiation signals and nucleotide diversity (π) metrics (top 5%), subsequent PCA clustering identified distinct spatial-scale genetic partitioning entirely consistent with the observed timeline trajectories across patches (**Fig 3b**).

We subsequently advanced these localized findings by engineering a comprehensive two-stage Bayesian hierarchical model, targeting the directed network influences in which spatial locations, driving genetic components (SNPs), transcriptome signatures, and secondary metabolomes determine major growth factors (leaf biomass) and reproductive traits (spikelet counts). Verification via robust linear regression comparisons between analytical posterior predictions and empirically observed distributions demonstrated high predictive fidelity across both multi-omics pathways (**Fig S3a**) and resultant phenotype assessments (**Fig S3b**). Thorough interpretation of causal interactions identified inside the established networking sequence illustrated strong hierarchical interdependencies. Fundamentally, structural habitat position imposed immediate deterministic control over corresponding genetics (_P(E > 0)_ = 1; **Fig 3c**), underlying transcriptome composition (_P(E > 0)_ = 1; **Fig 3d**), and resultant metabolomic structure (_P(E > 0)_ = 1; **Fig 3e**). Distinct molecular layers internally modulated downstream elements; the genetic framework elicited moderate influences guiding transcription behaviors (_P(E > 0)_ = 0.876; **Fig 3d**), while transcriptome variability robustly controlled shifting metabolomic structures (_P(E > 0)_ = 0.997; **Fig 3e**). However, the resulting connection parameters translating omics variances (both transcriptome and metabolome elements) directly onto targeted biomass growth traits (Transcriptome: _a_G_tran0_ = 0.2424, _P(E > 0)_ = 0.655; Metabolome: _b_G_met0_ = -0.5796, _P(E > 0)_ = 0.212) or reproductive outputs (Transcriptome: _a_R_tran0_ = -0.0852, _P(E > 0)_ = 0.409; Metabolome: _b_R_met0_ = -0.1213, _P(E > 0)_ = 0.402) presented mixed or weakly constrained pathway coefficients representing secondary or delayed system linkages (**Fig 3f–g**, **Table S4**). In summary, these integrated hierarchical assessments indicate that macrogeographic spatial conditions and initial establishment gradients directly structure early genetic architecture and cascade vigorously through cellular transcriptions and metabolomic interactions, thereby establishing a core foundation constraining broader, long-term phenotypic capabilities.
```

# Gemini质控评价
```
这是一份针对该手稿 Results 部分的详细审查报告。总体而言，该部分的数据整合非常紧密，但在语言表述上存在明显的“越界”问题——尤其是 **将贝叶斯统计模型的关联结果过度解释为“因果控制（causal interactions, deterministic control）”**，以及 **使用了大量具有明显 Discussion 倾向的主观或推测性词汇**。

以下是具体的审查结果和修改建议：

### 1. Overall assessment (整体评估)

Results 部分逻辑清晰，很好地顺应了“表型 -> 转录/代谢组 -> 遗传与多组学整合”的故事线。文中的引用图表（Figure 1-3, Table S3-S5等）与补充材料高度吻合，提取的贝叶斯模型系数与 Table S4 完全一致。  
**主要问题在于**：在描述分子层面的变化和建模结果时，使用了过多带有强烈机制性和推断性的词汇，跨越了“客观描述数据”的边界。此外，存在少量数据需要作者补充具体内容，且对代谢组剔除批次效应的描述过于模糊。

### 2. Evidence-boundary problems (证据边界问题)

1. **因果关系的过度宣称**：3.3 节中多次使用 “causal interactions（因果相互作用）”和 “deterministic control（决定性控制）”。贝叶斯分层模型（Bayesian hierarchical model）虽然能评估有向网络效应，但本质上仍是基于观测数据的统计推断，不包含功能验证实验。这里的 `P(E > 0) = 1` 仅代表后验概率支持正相关效应的置信度极高，不能等同于“决定性控制”。
2. **“适应性”与“重编程”的预设**：3.2 节中出现了 “modulating adaptative responses（调节适应性响应）”和 “metabolomic reprogramming（代谢重编程）”。“适应性”暗示了适合度（fitness）的提升，但实际上本研究仅观察了表达量变化；“重编程”暗示了一种主动的机制性切换。这些词不仅夸大事实，且属于典型的 Discussion 用语。
3. **批次效应（Batch effects）处理的掩饰性描述**：3.2 节中写道 “Following quality regulation through the directed exclusion of anomalous batch interactions”。对照 Methods 2.3 可知，实际操作是整体排除了第一批次的所有样本，保留了50个样本。原文描述过于委婉，应按客观事实陈述。

### 3. Discussion-like language to remove or downgrade (需删除或降级的讨论式用语)

- **3.1 节结论句**：“...undergo significant shifts in structural resource allocation and reproductive output traversing different colonization years.”
    - **建议**：用词尚可接受，但 "resource allocation" 略带理论性，可稍微简化。
- **3.2 节尾段**：“...reveals a highly structured functional shift governed by specific gene-regulatory programs modulating adaptative responses following patch establishment.”
    - **建议降级**：改为描述客观转录差异，如 "reveals distinct stage-specific transcriptomic profiles following patch establishment."
- **3.2 节代谢组尾段**：“...emphasize robust and tightly organized local metabolomic reprogramming across the chronosequence timeline.”
    - **建议降级**：去除 reprogramming，改为 "...demonstrate highly differentiated metabolomic profiles across the chronosequence."
- **3.3 节结尾引申**：“...cascade vigorously through cellular transcriptions and metabolomic interactions, thereby establishing a core foundation constraining broader, long-term phenotypic capabilities.”
    - **建议降级**：这是纯粹的 Discussion 结尾段。需要客观陈述："...showing strong sequential associations across genomic, transcriptomic, and metabolomic layers, though structural omics variation exhibited relatively weak constraints on final phenotypic traits."

### 4. Number/statistics issues (数字与统计一致性问题)

1. **数据库注释数据来源**：3.2 节中提到 “...49,126 tightly co-annotated core genes” 和 “identified a total of 73,711 expressed genes”。这些绝对数值在 Methods 或表单中并未被列出（Table S3 只列出了 mapping rate）。可以保留，但需作者最后核对这些由分析 pipeline 直出的统计数值是否准确。
2. **聚类基因数目验证**：四类时间表达趋势的驱动基因数为：`12,026 + 10,033 + 5,453 + 10,196 = 37,708` 个。请作者确认该子集与上文 49,126 或 73,711 之间的逻辑关系是准确无误的。
3. **模型参数漏洞**：3.3 节详细列举了模型结果并匹配了 Fig 3 和 Table S4 的参数。但遗漏了 Fig 3e (genetics → metabolome) 的直接影响通径（Table S4 中 `e_gen_met0 = -0.0794, P(>0)=0.348`）。最好补充一笔“遗传因素对代谢的直接影响较弱”，以保证结果段落对模型的还原是完整的。

### 5. Missing placeholders or author confirmation points (缺失的占位符与需确认点)

1. **[Data Required]**：这是极其关键的占位符。在 **3.2 节**中出现了两处，作者必须在提交前，根据 Figure 2c-f (GO) 和 Figure 2g (KEGG) 实际富集出来的高光通路名称（如 photosynthesis, secondary metabolite biosynthesis 等生物学过程）进行替换。在此不要盲目用编造词语填充。
2. **采样分区的对应问题**：在 3.1 节开头提到了“across three microgeographic zones (estuary, competition, and dense zones) (Fig 1a, Fig S1a)”，这是完全符合 Methods 记载的。然而，之后的分析全是依靠“colonization years (年份)”开展的（Figure 1b-e 等）。作者需确保“微地理区域”并未与“定殖年份”产生严重的共线性冲突，且在逻辑上不至于让读者疑惑为什么只提分区而不分析分区表型。

### 6. Revised sentences or revision suggestions (逐段修改建议示例)

以下是针对几个具有修改必要的问题段落进行“净化”处理（去主观化与去因果化）的推荐文本：
```
